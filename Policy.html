User Policy

1. Any major violation of school policy is violation of Informare policy. Examples: Expulsion, Suspension, and Etc.


2. Any attempt to alter the functions of Informare's accounts or servers is a violation of policy. Examples: Stealing Accounts, Spamming Server, and Creating Alt Accounts.


3. Any attacking or interfering with the work of Modearators, Daily Messanger, and Admin(Owner).


Staff Member policy

1. Staff members can not ban users for unjust reasons, and they can not ban for an unreasonable amount of time.


2. Can not try to take other staff members roles or try to overthrow Admin.


3. Being disrespectful to other staff is a violation of policy.
